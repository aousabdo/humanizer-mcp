Add Humanizer to Claude

A free tool that scores your writing for "this sounds AI-written" and suggests fixes.

What this does: Once added, you can paste any text into Claude and ask "score this for AI tells" — Claude will give you a 0–100 risk score and tell you which words and phrases to change.

Time to set up: About 2 minutes. You'll download one file and upload it once.

What you need: A free Claude account at claude.ai. That's it. No software to install, no terminal, no settings to configure.
1
Download the file

Click this link and save the file to your Downloads folder:

Download
github.com/aousabdo/humanizer-mcp/raw/main/skill/humanizer-mcp.zip

You'll get a small file called humanizer-mcp.zip (about 5 KB). Don't unzip it.

2
Open Claude and find the Skills page

Go to claude.ai in your web browser and sign in. Then click on your profile picture in the top right and follow this path:

Settings → Capabilities → Skills

(On some plans the menu may say "Skills" directly under Settings, or it may be called "Custom Capabilities" — pick whichever is shown.)

3
Upload the file

Click Upload skill (or the "+" button), pick the humanizer-mcp.zip file you just downloaded, and click Open.

Claude will process it for a few seconds and confirm. Done.

4
Try it

Open any Claude chat and paste a paragraph of text. Then say:

Score this for AI tells and tell me what to change.

Claude returns a score from 0 to 100, the specific words and phrases that look AI-written, and concrete suggestions for what to replace them with. It works in regular chats — you don't need to do anything special each time.

Other things you can ask:

  • "Run a quick scan on this paragraph and tell me which words sound AI."
  • "Compare these two drafts. Did my edit make it sound more human?"
  • "What words should I avoid if I don't want this to sound AI-generated?"

Where this works

The skill is tied to your Claude account, so it works automatically in claude.ai (the website), the Claude desktop app, and the Claude for Chrome extension. Set it up once on the website and it appears everywhere.

If something goes wrong

Don't see a "Skills" or "Capabilities" section? Anthropic is rolling this feature out gradually. Some accounts have it under Settings → Feature Preview. If it's not there at all, your account doesn't support custom skills yet — let the person who sent you this know, and they'll set you up with a different version.

Upload fails? Make sure you uploaded the .zip file, not an unzipped folder. Don't double-click the zip to extract it first; just upload the zip directly.

Claude doesn't seem to use the skill? Try being more direct: "Use the humanizer skill to score this for AI tells."
